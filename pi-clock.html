Raspberry Pi Digital Clock

This was my very first project with the Raspberry Pi and I created a Digital Clock for my room that also acted as a weather bug that could tell me the indoor temperature as well as the outdoor temperature and humidity.

Not only the Pi, but also I used an Arduino UNO and a temperature sensor plus a humidity sensor to gather the local information and display it using Tkinter.

This application consisted of several functions. It read the Indoor and Outdoor temperatures as well as the outdoor humidity. It gave a forecast of the local weather. Told me vocally weater or not I would need a jacket for the day. And of course gave me the time in 12-hour format.
