Other Hobbies

I wouldn't be a true Crossfitter unless I added it into my own portfolio about computer programming. Ever since I started programmin I also started working out. Both hobbies became a very large part of life. I was living proof that the nerd who sat behind a computer and wrote code could also be a strong, fast, and competitive athlete.

Crossfit was my outlet for stress and any problems I had and programming became my creative outlet. Since my begining of Crossfit I have partaken in a dozen of competitions and placed in the Top 10 in almost all of them. My best finish only being third though. This has not discouraged me though and I still train today for a competition in May of 2019.
